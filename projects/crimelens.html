Challenges
& Solutions

Data Normalization

Historical regional datasets frequently contain missing cells, and raw counts bias risk forecasts towards high-population states.

Challenge: Missing Dataset records & Demographic Skews

Raw crime sums automatically flagged populous states (like Uttar Pradesh) as high-risk, failing to reflect per-capita realities. Additionally, empty cells across years blocked model training algorithms.

Fix: KNN Imputation & Per-Capita Rate Normalization

We designed a pre-processing pipeline using Scikit-learn. Missing historical data cells are imputed using K-Nearest Neighbors algorithms based on neighboring state indexes. To correct demographic skews, crime numbers are normalized per 100,000 citizens. We also weighted crimes by violent severity coefficients rather than counting all offences equally, delivering fair, per-capita risk measurements.
